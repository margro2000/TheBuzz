About

The Buzz is rethinking the way we do digital media. Traditional media is changing rapidly, with many household names struggling to keep up with the changing consumer demand for digital content. The Buzz empowers individual content creators at Georgia Tech to pursue the stories they are passionate about. We operate with the assumption of innovation by creating immersive digital experiences, fostering an open dialogue, and exploring multimedia forms. We pursue stories in areas like tech, culture, and politics. We are currently looking to create a founding team of passionate, creative, generally kick-ass individuals.

Application

Want to be part of a diverse student group that values open dialogue, cutting-edge ideas, and creative expression? Join the founding team of The Buzz, a student group that produces digital media content. At The Buzz, we do things differently. We empower our content creators with a platform while providing them the space to explore the stories they are passionate about and take creative risks. We explore multimedia forms of communication and place value on hearing all perspectives. There is no better time to get involved, please fill this form to apply.


Tech Team Leader
In charge of managing all things technical- like maintaining the website’s front-end and back-end, building innovative UI experiences, and generally managing the building and launch of the site. Experience in web-dev is recommended!

Marketing Team Leader
In charge of getting the campus to know about The Buzz. This includes recruiting new members but also creating newsletters and ways to get traction for the website. You’ll get to think creatively and manage marketing campaigns, as well as work closely with the design team to develop a cohesive brand.

Operations and Growth Leader
In charge of thinking strategically about how The Buzz operates. This means managing and communicating meetings, but also thinking about how to consistently improve, incorporating new innovations, and thinking about direction.

Design Team Leader
At The Buzz, we love great design. If you have a passion for UI/UX design and want to be involved in creating a uniquely branded experience from scratch, this position might be a great fit.

Editing Lead
The editing lead gets to focus on content. You will work closely with the content creators, marketing, and operations team in order to make the best quality content on our site.

Content Creators
The content creators are what make The Buzz unique. We give content creators the freedom to pursue stories and passion projects that interest them. Content creators can use multi-media forms like video, audio, or create dynamic digital experiences. You will make up the creative hub of our site.
